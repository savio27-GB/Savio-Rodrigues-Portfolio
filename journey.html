My Journey in Aerospace Engineering

My journey into aerospace engineering began with a fascination for flight that dates back to my childhood. I remember being mesmerized by the sight of airplanes soaring through the sky, wondering how something so massive could defy gravity and stay aloft. This curiosity led me to explore the science behind flight and sparked a lifelong passion for understanding the mechanics of aviation. As I grew older, I dove into subjects like physics and mathematics, eager to uncover the principles that govern the world of aerospace. These subjects became the foundation of my academic path, ultimately guiding me towards a degree in aerospace engineering.
