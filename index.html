About Me

A dedicated aerospace professional with over three years of experience in technical records management, airworthiness compliance, and customer support within the aviation industry. Skilled in managing "Back-To-Birth" (BTB) investigations, handling technical documentation, and ensuring compliance with EASA Part-M and Part-145 regulations. Known for strong attention to detail, problem-solving abilities, and effective communication, with a commitment to maintaining high standards in documentation and compliance processes.

Services

Airworthiness Compliance Support

I provide full support with Airworthiness Directives (ADs), guiding you through compliance with the latest FAA, EASA, and UKCAA requirements to keep your fleet airworthy and safe.

Service Bulletin Implementation

From initial assessment to modification planning, I handle all aspects of Service Bulletins (SBs) to ensure smooth, efficient implementation within your operations.

Technical Documentation & Task Engineering

I develop precise, compliant work instructions and technical documentation for ADs, SBs, STCs, and modification kits to support your maintenance tasks and regulatory compliance.

Maintenance Program Support

I help review and update Aircraft Maintenance Programs (AMPs), aligning them with the Maintenance Planning Document (MPD) and ensuring your fleet operates at optimal safety and efficiency.

Continuous Improvement & Reliability Analysis

I identify opportunities for improvement within maintenance processes, helping clients reduce delays, enhance compliance, and improve overall operational reliability.
